Bev Oda ^NOT Annotation Bookmarklet

The Power

The document editing power embodied in this deceptively simple tool was previously only available to Federal Cabinet Ministers and staff. Now, you can negate any statement on any web page anywhere with the simple click of a mouse.

How to use it

  1. Drag the above link into your browser's bookmark toolbar, or right-click on the link and add it as a bookmark.
  2. Browse to any page on the web.
  3. Click the bookmark you added in step 1
  4. Now click anywhere on the page to insert a negating annotation.
  5. Click on an annotation to remove it.

Example

You can try it out on this page. Just click on the link, then choose a spot in the following text to insert a ^NOT.

RECOMMENDATION — That you sign below to indicate you do approve a contribution of $7,098,758 over four years for the above program.
